Creamy Italian Chicken Salad

marinated mozzarella & tomatoes, Parmesan frico, pine nuts
10 min 570 kcal

This salad has everything you need—and then some. Crisp leaf lettuce and tender cooked chicken are tossed in a dairy-free lemon aïoli and serve as the base of the salad. Fresh mozzarella and grape tomatoes, marinated in balsamic vinegar, add the next rich and tangy layer. Luscious Parmesan frico and earthy pine nuts garnish the quick-to-the-table meal.
1. Mix spaghetti sauce, seasoning, cream cheese, and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl (if making how reserve some of the Parmesan to sprinkle on before heating)
2. Add shredded chicken and mix till smooth
3. Serve with crackers or veggies or on a roll.
