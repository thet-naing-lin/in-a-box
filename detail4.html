Hot Honey Kale & Prosciutto Grain Bowls

ricotta, dried cranberries, lemon-chive vinaigrette, pecans
10 min 700 kcal

Crunchy, creamy, savory, and spicy, this hearty grain bowl has it all! You’ll dress kale in a lemon-chive vinaigrette, then toss in tart dried cranberries and a warm mix of grains—brown rice, barley, and red and white quinoa. Top with prosciutto, dollops of creamy ricotta cheese, and a sprinkle of crunchy, nutty pecans for a symphony of delicious flavors and satisfying textures. The finishing touch is a luxurious drizzle of hot honey butter that comes together in a flash.
